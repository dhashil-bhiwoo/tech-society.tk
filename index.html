The UTM Tech Society is a group dedicated to promoting interest in computing and information technology with great wit and panache. Join our little community of fellow students of the University of Technology, Mauritius and share the passion for tech.

   

The UTM Tech Society is built on the principles of sharing, collaborating and participating. Each member is encouraged to express their opinions and are free to bring ideas, suggestions and projects to the discussion table.

The Aims/Mission/Goals of the UTM Tech Society:

- Provide a platform for technology enthusiast to meet and share their passion.

- Conduct regular meetings, workshop and seminars on existing/emerging technologies

- Increase awareness of information technology among the community.

- Provide opportunity to members to plan, organize and execute any activity/project they have in mind which are in line with our goals.

- Improving the knowledge of students in the field of computer science, information and communication technology

- Provide a medium through which students can share their ideas, engage in activities which can help them gain skills vital to their academic success and personal development.
